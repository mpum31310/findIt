We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

5. Your Rights (POPI Act Compliance)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPI Act) of South Africa, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information

We collect information about children only as provided by their parents/guardians. This information is used solely for the purpose of managing items assigned to children.
