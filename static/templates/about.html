About Scanofinder

What is Scanofinder?

Scanofinder is a revolutionary app designed to help parents and guardians keep track of their children's belongings using QR code technology. Never lose an item again!

How It Works

  1. Create an account and subscribe for just R200 per year
  2. Add your children to your profile
  3. Create items and generate unique QR codes with descriptive labels
  4. Print and attach QR codes to your items
  5. When someone finds your item, they can scan the QR code and send you a message

Features

  • Generate QR codes with descriptive text labels
  • Manage multiple children and their items
  • Upload photos of your items
  • Receive messages from finders via QR code scanning
  • Track all your items in one place
